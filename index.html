Get to know us!

Rawfood café is a unique city centre organic and vegan friendly café situated in Temple Bar. Serving up to everything from organically farmed fair trade coffee to freshly made sandwiches also serving freshly prepared smoothies, juices with a scope of protein or bone broth powder, for those customers after using the gym in Temple Bar to nourish their body after exercise. So, whether you are a vegan or vegetarian or considering of keeping slim or taking care of your health Rawfood Café is the right choice.
